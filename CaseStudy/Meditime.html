THE PROBLEM

50% of people do not take the Medicine properly

Source: www.verywellhealth.com

People often face difficulties with their medication, like worrying about side effects, being scared of taking the wrong amount, and feeling anxious about using it the wrong way. Even if someone is good at taking medicine regularly, they might forget to take the right medicine at the right time. Even though taking medicine seems simple, about 50% of people don't do it right—they don't follow the instructions for how much to take, when to take it, and sometimes don't bother with how to use it correctly. In some cases, people even take too much or too little of the prescribed dose.
